## Requirements

You can find the basic requirements in the official PDF on the exchange program's website. However, the key requirements are:

- Must be an Indonesian citizen,
- Aged 18-35,
- A university graduate with a major related to IT,
- Currently working in IT.

Additional requirements from Swift Management AG:

- 1-2 years of experience creating websites with **React**. Experience with **Next.js** or **Remix** is a plus.
- Fluent in English, both verbal and written.
- Willing to stay in Basel, Switzerland for approximately 6 months.
- Open to learning basic German.
- Eager to learn new things and enhance your development skills.
- Not a senior developer. If you have over 3 years of experience as a software developer, please do not apply.

## What to Expect

- The opportunity to work alongside Swiss professionals and experience life in Switzerland.

- A potential extension of your stay beyond the initial agreement.
- The exact starting date is subject to the bureaucratic process.
